ArcticFoxAlpha Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

ArcticFoxAlpha (the "App") values and respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle and protect your data.

The App is designed primarily for iPad/iOS and follows a local-first architecture. Data-source keys and AI service keys configured in the App are managed by users themselves, and online requests are sent directly from users' devices to selected third-party providers.

1. Local-First Data Storage

  1. The App follows a local-first architecture. Strategy settings, backtesting records, model configurations, and data-source settings are stored locally on your device by default (for example, in SQLite or local files).
  2. We do not proactively upload the above local data to our servers, unless cloud-sync functionality is introduced in the future with your explicit consent.
  3. You may back up, export, or delete relevant data locally at your discretion.

2. Data We May Process

  1. Data you provide: such as data-source API keys and AI service API keys, used solely for requests initiated directly by your device to third-party services.
  2. Network request data: when you use online features such as market data, news, or AI analysis, the App sends only the necessary request parameters to third-party providers you configure.
  3. Diagnostic information (optional): if you enable debugging or submit feedback, this may include technical information such as error logs, HTTP status codes, and timestamps. It does not include sensitive credentials such as account passwords or payment instruments, unless you provide them voluntarily in your submission.

3. Third-Party AI Analysis Services (Important)

  1. When you use AI interpretation or AI analysis features, the App sends the parameters strictly necessary to generate analysis results to the AI provider you configure in the App.
  2. These requests are sent directly from your device. We do not host your API keys and do not maintain a standalone chat-history database.
  3. Data handling by third-party providers is governed by their own privacy policies and terms of service, which you should review before use.

4. Data Sources and Compliance

  1. Market data, news, and related information may come from third-party data services (including interfaces you configure).
  2. You are responsible for complying with third-party service terms, licensing scope, and regulatory requirements.
  3. We do not sell your personal data, do not use it for targeted advertising, and do not share local business data with unrelated third parties.

U.S. Equities Information-Service Boundary

  1. Information and AI-generated outputs provided by the App are for informational and research-support purposes only, and do not constitute investment advice, promises, or guarantees of returns.
  2. Financial markets involve substantial risk. You must make independent decisions and bear the consequences of those decisions.
  3. We are not liable for trading losses arising from third-party data interruptions, delays, errors, rate limits, or access-policy changes.

5. Permissions and Security

  1. The App uses permissions (such as network access and local file access) only to the extent necessary for core functionality.
  2. Please safeguard your device and locally stored keys against unauthorized access.
  3. If your device is lost, compromised, or you suspect key leakage, replace relevant third-party service keys immediately.

6. Minors

If you are under 18 years old, please review and use the App under the guidance of a parent or legal guardian.

7. Policy Updates

  1. We may update this policy as required by product evolution or applicable law.
  2. Updated versions will be published in the App or on project pages.
  3. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
